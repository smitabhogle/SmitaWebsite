Advanced French

At Mass Academy, language classes are taught by Madame Wildfong . I took French at my previous school, so I already had a solid foundation in French before coming to MAMS. This is an immersion language class, meaning that we learn through watching movies, listening to music, and playing games, such as Monopoly and Clue, in French.

French Baking!

One project we recently completed was our French Cooking Project. Everyone chose a recipe from a French speaking country to make and bring into school for a pot luck. There was a diverse selection of foods from many different countries. I chose to make Bûche De Noël au Mousse au Chocolat, a French Christmas cake. Below is my presentation!
