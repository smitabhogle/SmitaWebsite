Humanities

At Mass Academy, Humanities is taught by Ms. Small. Humanities is a combination of an English and History class. The essential question of this course is "What does it mean to be human?" We look at a variety of different forms of literature and focus on how humans and society have changed throughout time. One of my favorite activities that we do in Humanities is class wide discussions. I think it's very interesting to be able to hear eveyone's unique perspective on different tests and topics.

DBQ Essay

During C Term, we all worked in groups to prepare a DBQ question along with sources. Then, we got to choose from any of our classmates' prompts to write an essay. My essay focused on the effects of tourism.

Arcadia Motif Presentation

One of my favorite stories we read in HUM was Arcadia. This novel switches back and forth between two time periods, and uses many unique motifs in order to connect the characters actions to over arching themes. My group focused on the Apple and Dahlia motifs in the book. To the left is our presentation!
