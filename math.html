Math

At Mass Academy, the math course that we take is Mathematical Modeling, which is taught by Mr. Regele. Math class at MAMS is very different compared to traditional high school math classes. Instead of following a textbook, we focus on application math problems relating to real life scenarios. Mathematical Modeling has a heavy emphasis on collaboration, we even get new table groups each week in order to have the opportunity to work with a variety of different people. The table groups are determined by drawing a random card as you enter the room at the start of the week and sitting with the people who draw the same suite as you.

HiMCM Math Competition

In November, all the juniors (and some seniors) at Mass Academy practiced in the HiMCM math challenge. HiMCM is a mathematical modeling competition where groups have 36 hours to complete a report to solve a real life problem.
